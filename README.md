# 🧠 Business Understanding

Semiconductors are the foundation of modern technology, powering smartphones, AI systems, automobiles, data centers, defense systems, and industrial automation.

Countries like Taiwan and the Netherlands dominate critical areas of the semiconductor ecosystem, while India is rapidly investing in becoming a global semiconductor hub.

This project aims to analyze:
- Global semiconductor market trends
- Country-wise manufacturing capabilities
- Export and revenue comparisons
- Government investments
- Supply chain dependencies
- Future growth opportunities for India

---

# ❓ Problem Statement

The semiconductor industry is one of the most strategically important industries in the world, yet manufacturing and technological capabilities are heavily concentrated in a few countries.

This project attempts to answer:
- Can India become a semiconductor manufacturing leader?
- How does India compare with Taiwan and the Netherlands?
- Which countries dominate different semiconductor sectors?
- What trends define the future semiconductor ecosystem?

---

# 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze semiconductor industry growth globally
- Compare India, Taiwan, Netherlands, USA, and China

# 🔍 Key Insights

- Taiwan dominates advanced semiconductor fabrication.
- The Netherlands plays a critical role through ASML lithography systems.
- India has strong potential due to government initiatives and engineering talent.
- AI growth is significantly increasing semiconductor demand.
- Supply chain concentration creates geopolitical risks.
